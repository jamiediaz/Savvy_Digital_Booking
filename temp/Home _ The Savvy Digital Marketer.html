Our Process

Discovery Phase

We learn everything there is to know about your business, customers, competitors and industry. We work closely with you to understand your business goals and vision and present a proposal detailing scope of work, project timeline and deliverables tailored to your needs. 

Strategy & Planning

With the data and information received from the discovery phase, we create a strategic plan that includes campaign objective, mock-up creative and deliverables, schedule and KPI’s. The plan will then be evaluated by you and other key project stakeholders. We will only move forward to the next phase once we receive your alignment and approval.

Development

Once strategic plan is approved, we will work on developing all assets, content, deliverables and ad accounts  in line with agreed timeline.

Execution

During execution phase, all campaigns and projects will be launched as we keep to schedule, and make sure the execution of the project matches up to the initial plan.

Optimize & Measure

The last phase is a continual process that involves measuring the performance of the campaigns or strategy based on KPI’s set in planning phase. We will provide reporting to the proper stakeholders and optimize ads and creative as we see fit over the life of the campaign.
